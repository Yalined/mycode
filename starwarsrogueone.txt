2016: Rogue One: A Star Wars Story
Rated PG-13 2h 13m - Director: Gareth Edwards - IMDb- 7.8/10

* Its place in the Star Wars time line is just before A New Hope. It is also 5 years after the Star Wars Rebels television show.

* Visual effects legend John Knoll came up with the story. He pitched it to Kathleen Kennedy and the rest is history

* The firing sequence is copied exactly from that of ANH, frame for frame.

* Rogue One is the first Star Wars movie in which no one mentions the name “Skywalker.”

* Rogue One was shipped to theaters under the name “Los Alamos.”
