2018: Black Panther
Rated PG-13 2h 14m - Director: Ryan Coogler - IMDb- 7.3/10

*  The name of the fictional land Wakanda was given after the Wakamba (aka Kamba) tribe inhabiting Kenya.

* The film was released in February, which is officially marked as Black History month across U.S. and Canada.

* Adoption of black cats have been reportedly increased after the film release. Many are naming their cats after the character names.

* The fighting in Black Panther is based on Capoeira, which is an African-Brazilian martial art.

* Black Panther was the first marvel movie to get Academy Awards.
